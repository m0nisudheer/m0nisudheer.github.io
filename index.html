About Me

Hello there, my name is Mani Sudheer

I am a graduate of Bapatla Engineering College with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering, achieving an 8.6 CGPA in 2024. Throughout my academic journey, I actively participated in various technical events and competitions, which helped build a strong foundation in my field. Coming from a close-knit family in the East Godavari district, my parents have always encouraged me to pursue my dreams. In my free time, I enjoy reading books and playing cricket. My strengths include being hard-working, self-motivated, and a quick learner, with a notable ability to adapt to new situations quickly. My short-term goal is to join a reputable company where I can apply my skills and grow professionally, ultimately aiming to achieve a respectable position within the organization.

During my academic journey, I completed internships in web development at Exposys Data Labs and cybersecurity at Spypro Security Solutions. I also worked on two significant projects: enhancing a brain tumor detection model using deep learning (CNN and VGG16 architecture), achieving an impressive accuracy of 98.3%, and developing an online bookstore focused on reducing spoilage of subjective books and ensuring longevity for readers. Additionally, I have honed my development skills and problem-solving abilities through several mini-projects.
